Transpoly

Experimenting with CSS polygon transitions

Inspiration: species-in-pieces.com
Polygonisation of reference images* with: DMesh - Triangulation Image Generator
  • version 1
    Animation of polygons from one state to another
  • version 2
    Rendering of OBJ file, including interpolation of the polygon color from an image
  • version 3
    All shards entering uniformly from the left side
  • version 4
    Shards entering from random sides (left/right) - super slow!
  • version 5
    Shards expanding from their respective center point.
  • version 6
    Transitioning between two images (by looping over the shards, starting with the smaller image).
